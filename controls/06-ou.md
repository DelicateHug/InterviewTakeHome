# [06] OU InterviewTakeHome

**Type:** Organizational Unit

> **In plain terms —** The folder that holds the workload account. The S3 guardrail SCP [07] and the deny-S3-outside-org RCP [08] attach here, so every account inside automatically inherits them.

## Controls applied

- **Prevention:** Isolation boundary `ou-33e3-5p8xygxw`; SCP [07] + RCP [08] attach here only, so existing accounts are untouched.
- **Detection:** Org CloudTrail on policy attach/detach.
- **Alert:** Attach / detach → change-alerter [40].

## What would trigger an alert

- An admin attaches or detaches an SCP / RCP on the OU (weakening a guardrail) → change-alerter [40] → SNS [36].
- An account is moved into or out of the OU → change-alerter [40].
