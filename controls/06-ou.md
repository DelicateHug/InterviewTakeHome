# [06] OU InterviewTakeHome

**Type:** Organizational Unit

> **In plain terms —** The folder that holds the workload account. The S3 guardrail SCP [[07]](07-scp.md) and the deny-S3-outside-org RCP [[08]](08-rcp.md) attach here, so every account inside automatically inherits them.

## Controls applied

- **Prevention:**
  - Isolation boundary `ou-33e3-5p8xygxw`
  - SCP [[07]](07-scp.md) + RCP [[08]](08-rcp.md) attach here only, so existing accounts are untouched.
- **Detection:** Org CloudTrail on policy attach/detach.
- **Alert:** Attach / detach → change-alerter [[40]](40-change-alerter.md).

## What would trigger an alert

- An admin attaches or detaches an SCP / RCP on the OU (weakening a guardrail) → change-alerter [[40]](40-change-alerter.md) → SNS [[36]](36-sns.md).
- An account is moved into or out of the OU → change-alerter [[40]](40-change-alerter.md).
